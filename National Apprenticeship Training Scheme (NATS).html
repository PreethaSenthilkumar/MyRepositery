Dear Student,

Please ensure fulfillment of following eligibility criteria for further consideration of the students candidature by the training Establishment as an Apprentice under The Apprentices Act (herein after referred as "Act"). The following table provides Eligibility criteria for students based on their education qualification along with the documents/ information / data that are required before starting the enrolment process.

Required Documents for Enrollment

Educational Qualification

  1. A degree in engineering or technology granted by statutory University.
  2. A degree in engineering or technology granted by an Institutions empowered to grant such degrees by an Act of Parliament.
  3. Graduate examination of Provisional bodies recognized by the Central Government as equivalent to a degree.
  4. A sandwich course student who is under-going training in order that he may hold a degree in engineering or technology as mentioned at (a) and (b) above

Document / information Required

  1. University Regn. No / Roll. No/ Enrol. No
  2. List of elective subjects studied during Degree in Engineering / Technology
  3. Details of additional short term / certificate courses
  4. Provisional Degree Certificate / Consolidated Mark sheet

Required Documents for Enrollment

Educational Qualification

  1. A diploma in engineering or technology granted by a State Council or Board of Technical Education established by a State Government.
  2. A diploma in engineering or technology granted by a University.
  3. A diploma in engineering or technology granted by an Institute recognized by the State Government or Central Government as equivalent to (a) and (b) above.
  4. A sandwich course student who is under-going training in order that he may hold a diploma mentioned in (a), (b) and (c) above.

Document / information Required

  1. Regn. No / Roll. No/ Enrol. No issued by DOTE / DTE / SBTE/University/diploma granting Institute
  2. List of elective subjects studied during Diploma in Engineering / Technology
  3. Details of additional short term / certificate courses
  4. Details of additional short term / certificate coursesd. Provisional Diploma Certificate / Consolidated Mark sheet

Educational Qualification

Graduate Engineers, Diploma Engineers certificate holders are eligible for registration as Apprentices provided their Apprenticeship contract forms are registered by BOATs / BOPT within a period of 3 years from the date of passing.

Terms and Conditions

  1. This is merely an enrollment for Apprenticeship Training, Students have to be proactive and look for advertisement for Centralised Selection published in leading newspapers on a routine basis by BOATs / BOPT. The same information will also be displayed in this Website.
  2. Though it is mandatory for the Industries / Establishments to engage Apprentices, it is not mandatory for the industries to select students only through our office. Therefore students should try various sources to get Apprenticeship Training.
  3. If you have already been recruited through campus selection for regular job and if you possess work experience of one year or more,you should not enroll for Apprenticeship Training.
  4. Students are eligible for Apprenticeship Training within three years of their passing their qualifying examination. Only students who have passed their qualifying examination can register for Apprenticeship Training.
  5. All Communication from BOATs / BOPT will be through E-mail, you may lose the opportunity for Apprenticeship Training, if you do not provide one, so kindly provide Email ID.
  6. Multiple/duplicate entries of student enrollment will not be considered for apprenticeship training.
  7. Students who have completed Post Graduate qualification in Engineering (M.E / M.Tech / MBA) are not eligible for Apprenticeship training.
  8. After successful enrollment in our web Portal, students are not required to come to our office for certificate verification and their original certificates will be verified only at the time of selection by the employer.
  9. Selection of Apprentices is the employer's prerogative.
  10. Before filling the application, you are requested to go through Audio / Video preview which is available in our website that gives you an idea to complete the registration easily.
  11. Please be aware that all the important communication will be sent through your Email ID. Ensure entered Email ID is valid.
